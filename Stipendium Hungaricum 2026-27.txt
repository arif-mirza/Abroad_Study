🎯 Practical Flow (Stipendium Hungaricum 2026-27, Bachelor CS)
🟢 Step 1: Abhi se (Aug 2025 tak)

Passport banwao (sabse pehle) → bina passport application hi submit nahi hogi.

Educational Documents attestation

Matric + Inter → IBCC se attested.

Copies nikal lo + scan kar lo.

USAT-CS Test ke liye prep start karo.

Pehla test HEC Feb/March 2025 me hoga, apply HEC portal se.
🔗 USAT Info – HEC (https://www.hec.gov.pk/english/HECAnnouncements/Pages/USAT.aspx)

Soft Copies banani shuru karo (PDF/JPG format).

====================================================================================================================
🟡 Step 2: 2025 (Sep – Nov)

USAT ka Result ready rakho (60%+ aim).

Motivation Letter likho (kyun CS, kyun Hungary, aur wapas aake Pakistan ke liye kya karoge).

Medical Certificate banao (simple se government hospital/clinic se).

====================================================================================================================

🔵 Step 3: Application Time (Nov 2025 – Jan 2026)

Dono portals par apply karna:

🔗 Hungarian Portal (https://apply.stipendiumhungaricum.hu/)

🔗 HEC Portal (https://scholarship.hec.gov.pk)

Upload karne wale documents:

Passport (must)

CNIC/B-Form

Matric + Inter Certificates (attested)

Transcript/Marksheet (attested)

Motivation Letter

Medical Certificate

Police Character Certificate (kabhi kabhi manga jata hai)

USAT Score Card

Submit before 15 Jan 2026 (Hungary) & 20 Jan 2026 (HEC)
====================================================================================================================


🟣 Step 4: Selection & Visa (Feb – Sep 2026)

Feb–Apr 2026 → HEC shortlist karega, phir Hungary universities ka entrance exam (online).

May–July 2026 → Offer letter aayega.

July–Aug 2026 → Visa process Hungarian Embassy Islamabad.

Sep 2026 → Fly to Hungary ✈️🇭🇺

📑 Documents Priority Order

👉 Pehle banane/ready karne wale:

Passport

Matric + Inter Certificates (IBCC Attestation)

CNIC/B-Form

👉 Baad me banane wale:
4. USAT Score Card (2025 me attempt)
5. Motivation Letter (2025 me likhna)
6. Medical Certificate (application ke time se thoda pehle)
7. Police Character Certificate (agar demand ho)
